Introduction to Computers

A computer is an electronic device that accepts data, processes it, stores it, and produces information. Computers are used in schools, businesses, hospitals, banks, and many other places.

Parts of a Computer

  • Monitor
  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Central Processing Unit (CPU)
  • Printer

Uses of Computers

  • Education and research
  • Communication
  • Entertainment
  • Business transactions
  • Data storage

Conclusion

Computers have become an important part of modern life. They help people work faster, communicate easily, and access information from around the world.
